# Code of Conduct

FrameBoard follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) principles.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about technical tradeoffs.
- Keep feedback focused on code, docs, behavior, and maintainability.
- Make space for new contributors to ask questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of project work or discussion.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the project maintainers through the security/contact channel listed in `SECURITY.md`.
